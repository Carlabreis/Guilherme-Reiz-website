About

Hello, my name is Guilherme, I'm Brazilian and live in Brazil. Currently, I work with digital illustration for editorial and advertising! Since I was little, I've been connected to art, I've always loved painting, drawing, music, crafts, etc. I remember in high school, I was not so good with the required subjects, and my notebooks were full of drawings, there were few things written, but lots of drawings. Nowadays, I usually say that there would be no other job in the world that would make me feel happy if not illustration. I am completely in love with what I do! I am constantly improving my techniques and skills, to deliver the best to my clients and partners. Hope we can work together soon!
